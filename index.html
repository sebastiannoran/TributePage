Dr. Paul Kalanithi Tribute

Author of "When Breathe Becomes Air"

"Dr. Kalanithi was born on April 1, 1977 in Bronxville, New York. After graduating from the University of Cambridge with a Masters of Philosophy Dr. Kalanithi attended the Yale School of Medicine. Dr. Kalanithi returned to Stanford University (undergraduate) to complete their Nuerosurgery residency program"
